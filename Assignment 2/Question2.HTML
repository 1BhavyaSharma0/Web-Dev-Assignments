LinkedIn is an online, business-focused social networking service used primarily for professional networking and career development.
Users create professional profiles that serve as digital resumes, connect with other professionals, find job opportunities, and share industry insights and articles.
It functions similarly to other social media platforms but with a focus on career growth and building professional relationships.

LeetCode is an online platform for coding interview preparation.
